The Flow

HOW A TRANSACTION WORKS

From buyer pays to everyone gets paid — automatic, transparent, on-chain verified.

01
Buyer Pays

Fan pays by credit card or USDC wallet. Funds lock immediately in escrow — not in MintSeat's account, not in the seller's account. Held securely until delivery.

02
Ticket Transfers

Seller transfers ticket through normal channels. MintSeat gets confirmation automatically from the venue system, or buyer taps "Confirm Received" for external tickets.

03
Splits Route

On confirmed delivery, payments route automatically: artist royalty, seller payout, platform fee — all in one settlement. No manual transfers. No waiting.

04
Everyone's Paid

Artist gets their 7%. Seller gets their money. MintSeat takes 1.5%. Buyer gets a verified receipt. If anything goes wrong — automatic full refund, no questions.
