How It Works

FROM SALE TO SETTLEMENT

Every transaction — primary or resale — flows through the same automatic process.

01
Fan Buys

Fan purchases through your existing checkout. MintSeat intercepts automatically via the embed — no redirect, no new checkout page.

02
secure payment hold Locks

Funds lock in secure payment hold immediately. Ticket registered as Partner Verified in MintSeat registry with your configured rules and royalty settings.

03
Delivery Confirmed

Ticket delivers. MintSeat confirms automatically via venue system or buyer confirmation. secure payment hold releases on confirmation.

04
Splits Route

On any resale: artist royalty routes automatically to their registered wallet. Seller gets paid. MintSeat takes 1.5%. Every time. No manual work.
